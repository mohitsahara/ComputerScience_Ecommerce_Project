Important Information
  • All orders are dispatched from our UK warehouse within 1 business day of order confirmation.
  • A tracking number will be sent to your registered email address once your order has been dispatched.
  • Deliveries are made Monday–Friday, 8am–6pm. A signature may be required for high-value items.
  • If you are not home at the time of delivery, the courier will leave a calling card with redelivery instructions.
  • TechMart is not responsible for delays caused by customs clearance for international shipments.
  • Remote area surcharges may apply for deliveries to the Scottish Highlands, Northern Ireland, and islands.
Packaging

All items are carefully packed in protective, branded TechMart packaging. High-value electronics are double-boxed with foam inserts. We use eco-friendly recyclable materials wherever possible.

Returns & Refunds Policy

30-Day Hassle-Free Returns
Change your mind? No problem.
What Can Be Returned
  • Items returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Products in their original, unopened packaging with all accessories and documentation included.
  • Faulty or damaged items (even if opened) — please contact us within 48 hours of receipt.
  • Items that do not match the product description on our website.
What Cannot Be Returned
  • Items that have been opened and used (unless faulty).
  • Products with broken seals on software or digital content.
  • Custom or personalised orders.
  • Items returned after the 30-day window.
How to Return an Item
  1. Contact our support team via the Contact Us page with your order number and reason for return.
  2. We will email you a pre-paid return shipping label within 1 business day.
  3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging and attach the label.
  4. Drop the parcel at your nearest post office or courier collection point.
  5. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 3–5 business days.
Refunds

Approved refunds are credited back to your original payment method:

  • Card payments: 3–5 business days after approval.
  • PayPal: 1–2 business days after approval.
  • Bank Transfer: 5–7 business days after approval.
  • Original shipping cost is refunded only if the return is due to our error or a faulty item.
